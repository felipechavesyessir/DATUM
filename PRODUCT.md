# DATUM Product Context

## Register

brand

## Product Purpose

DATUM is an institutional website for a premium geospatial intelligence company. The site should turn technical credibility into clear commercial confidence for engineering, GIS, cartography, LiDAR, drones, infrastructure, environmental, mining, logistics, and territorial analysis audiences.

## Users

- Engineering leaders evaluating territorial intelligence partners.
- GIS and geotechnology teams looking for reliable data workflows.
- Infrastructure, environmental, mining, and logistics operators who need traceable spatial decisions.
- Technical buyers who care about precision, auditability, field operations, and clean delivery.

## Brand Principles

- Precise, technical, reliable, contemporary, and premium.
- Reads as engineering and applied geospatial intelligence, not generic SaaS.
- Uses cartographic cues: contour lines, coordinate grids, layers, triangulation, datum references, evidence, and field-to-report traceability.
- Communication is short, direct, objective, technical, and trustworthy.

## Anti-References

- Generic AI startup language.
- Overly futuristic visual effects.
- Childlike software aesthetics.
- Marketing-heavy claims without technical substance.
- Decorative 3D, glow, bevel, heavy shadows, and arbitrary organic shapes.

## Quality Bar

Flagship institutional landing page. The page should feel designed, interactive, and production-ready, with strong responsive behavior and accessible interaction states.
